Have you ever been playing Minecraft and thought, "Man, I really want gun."
No?
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds a simple gun to your Minecraft world.
Using the crafting recipes above you can craft a gun.
To load the gun you right click once.
To shoot the gun you right click once.
So if you can click really fast, your enemies better watch out.
This gun shoots as fast as you can click.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack is intended for worlds with cheats enabled.
If something breaks try using /reload.
