---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds 11 new guns to your Minecraft world.
Each gun has it's own advantages and disadvantages.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TIP: You can hold down right click to fire the gun as fast as possible.
TIP: When crafting ammo, it doesn't matter where the ingredients go
just as long as you have the right ones. (same for med items)
TIP: All guns must be crafted in the EXACT order above.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pistol
-High firing speed
-Medium damage
-Medium range
Assault Rifle
-Very high firing speed
-Low damage
-Medium range
Shotgun
-Medium firing speed
-High damage
-Low range
Sniper Rifle
-Low firing speed
-High damage
-High range
Chemical Thrower
-High firing speed
-Only does poison damage
-Poison lasts for about 7 seconds.
-Very low range
Ink Blaster
-High firing speed
-No damage
-Inflicts slowness, weakness, and blindness to targets.
-Low range
Ray Gun: Green
-Medium firing speed
-Low damage
-Low range
-Doesn't require ammo
Ray Gun: Blue
-Low firing speed
-Medium damage
-Medium range
-Doesn't require ammo
Laser Pistol
-Medium firing speed
-High damage
-Medium range
Laser Rifle
-Low firing speed
-Very high damage
-High range
Laser Cannon
-Very low firing speed
-High damage
-Very high range
-Explodes on impact.
-Doesn't break blocks.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack also adds in 4 new healing items.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Medkit
-Gives regeneration 2 for a short period of time
-Gives instant health 1
-Clears all negative effects
Regeneration Syringe
-Gives regeneration 1 for a medium period of time
Absorption Syringe
-Gives 8 absorption hearts for 10 minutes.
Laser Shield
-Doubles your health for 10 minutes.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In addition to adding in guns and healing items,
this datapack also adds on 3 new mobs
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HandyBot
-Has 40 HP
-Deals medium damage
-The weakest of the 3
GuardBot
-Has 60 HP
-Deals high damage
-An overall strong mob
WarBot
-Has 80 HP
-Deals very high damage
-Has a short ranged laser gun that does medium damage.
-The strongest, be cautious when you see one.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you use the guns in creative mode they will still require and consume ammo.

The resourcepack is required.
