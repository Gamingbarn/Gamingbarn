------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds 15 new guns, 4 new medical items, and 4 new equipment items!
Each item has its own upsides and downsides.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You reload guns with shift + right click!
The amount of ammo left in your magazine is displayed
by the durability bar of the gun. Or in the gun's description.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TIP: You can hold down right click to fire the gun as fast as possible.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To see the crafting recipes for the guns you need to craft the Gun Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and an iron ingot the crafting grid to craft one.
To see the crafting recipes for the med items you need to craft the Medicine Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and an apple in the crafting grid to craft one.
To see the crafting recipes for the equipment you need to craft the Equipment Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and gunpowder in the crafting grid to craft one.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pistol
-High firing speed
-Medium damage
-Medium range
-Medium recoil
-16 shots per magazine
Assault Rifle
-Very high firing speed
-Low damage
-Medium range
-Low recoil
-32 shots per magazine
Shotgun
-Medium firing speed
-High damage
-Low range
-High recoil
-8 shots per magazine
-Shells are loaded 1 at a time
Sniper Rifle
-Low firing speed
-High damage
-High range
-Bullets pierce through entities
-Very high recoil
-4 shots per magazine
Chemical Thrower
-High firing speed
-Only does poison damage
-Poison lasts for about 7 seconds
-Cloud travels through entities
-Very low range
-No recoil
-24 shots per magazine
Ink Blaster
-High firing speed
-No damage
-Inflicts slowness, weakness, and blindness to targets
-Low range
-No recoil
-24 shots per magazine
Ray Gun: Green
-Medium firing speed
-Low damage
-Low range
-Doesn't require ammo
-No recoil
Ray Gun: Blue
-Low firing speed
-Medium damage
-Medium range
-Doesn't require ammo
-No recoil
Laser Pistol
-Medium firing speed
-High damage
-Medium range
-Medium recoil
-4 shots per magazine
Laser Rifle
-Low firing speed
-Very high damage
-High range
-Medium recoil
-4 shots per magazine
Laser Cannon
-Very low firing speed
-High damage
-Very high range
-Explodes on impact.
-Doesn't break blocks.
-Very high recoil
-1 shot per magazine
-Lengthy reloading time
Combat Shotgun
-High firing speed
-High damage
-Very low range
-High recoil
-16 shots per magazine
Bazooka
-Very low firing speed
-High damage
-Very high range
-Explodes on impact.
-Breaks Blocks
-Very high recoil
-1 shot per magazine
-Lengthy reloading time
Flame Thrower
-Very high firing speed
-Very low damage
-Very low range
-No Recoil
-50 shots per magazine
-Sets blocks and targets on fire
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Medkit
-Gives regeneration 2 for a short period of time
-Gives instant health 1
-Clears all negative effects
Regeneration Syringe
-Gives regeneration 1 for a medium period of time
Absorption Syringe
-Gives 8 absorption hearts for 10 minutes.
Bandage
-Heals 6 hp over 4 seconds
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
C4
-Drops to the ground when used
-Explodes after 10 seconds
Land Mine
-Drops to the ground when used
-Arms itself after 10 seconds
-Explodes when an entity touches it
Auto Turret
-Drops to the ground when used
-Will shoot nearby hostile mobs
-Lasts for 5 minutes
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can get all guns by running the command:
/function give:all_guns

You can get all ammo by running the command:
/function give:all_ammo

You can get all healing items by running the command:
/function give:all_meds

You can get all books by running the command:
/function give:all_books

You can get all equipment by running the command:
/function give:all_equipment
