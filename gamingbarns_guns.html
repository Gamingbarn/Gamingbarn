---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds 12 new guns to your Minecraft world.
Each gun has it's own advantages and disadvantages.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TIP: You can hold down right click to fire the gun as fast as possible.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To see the crafting recipes for the guns you need to craft the Gun Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and an iron ingot the crafting grid to craft one.
To see the crafting recipes for the med items you need to craft the Medicine Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and an apple in the crafting grid to craft one.
There is also a book you can craft that provides details stats on each gun.
You must place a book and a gunpowder in the crafting grid to craft one.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pistol
-High firing speed
-Medium damage
-Medium range
Assault Rifle
-Very high firing speed
-Low damage
-Medium range
Shotgun
-Medium firing speed
-High damage
-Low range
Sniper Rifle
-Low firing speed
-High damage
-High range
Chemical Thrower
-High firing speed
-Only does poison damage
-Poison lasts for about 7 seconds.
-Very low range
Ink Blaster
-High firing speed
-No damage
-Inflicts slowness, weakness, and blindness to targets.
-Low range
Ray Gun: Green
-Medium firing speed
-Low damage
-Low range
-Doesn't require ammo
Ray Gun: Blue
-Low firing speed
-Medium damage
-Medium range
-Doesn't require ammo
Laser Pistol
-Medium firing speed
-High damage
-Medium range
Laser Rifle
-Low firing speed
-Very high damage
-High range
Laser Cannon
-Very low firing speed
-High damage
-Very high range
-Explodes on impact.
-Doesn't break blocks.
Combat Shotgun
-High firing speed
-High damage
-Very low range
-High recoil
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack also adds in 4 new healing items.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Medkit
-Gives regeneration 2 for a short period of time
-Gives instant health 1
-Clears all negative effects
Regeneration Syringe
-Gives regeneration 1 for a medium period of time
Absorption Syringe
-Gives 8 absorption hearts for 10 minutes.
Laser Shield
-Doubles your health for 10 minutes.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In addition to adding in guns and healing items,
this datapack also adds on 3 new mobs
(These mobs are disabled by default)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HandyBot
-Has 40 HP
-Deals medium damage
-The weakest of the 3
GuardBot
-Has 60 HP
-Deals high damage
-An overall strong mob
WarBot
-Has 80 HP
-Deals very high damage
-Has a short ranged laser gun that does medium damage.
-The strongest, be cautious when you see one.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can get all guns by running the command:
/function give:all_guns

You can get all ammo by running the command:
/function give:all_ammo

You can get all healing items by running the command:
/function give:all_meds

You can get all books by running the command:
/function give:all_books

To enable the robot custom monsters you must run the command:
function config:robots_toggle_on

You can turn them back off with the command:
function config:robots_toggle_off

(These are for players with cheats only)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you use the guns in creative mode they will still require and consume ammo.

The resourcepack is required.
