This data pack adds in 3 brand new magic items to the game.
Each item is very strong however, you must do a few thing before you can use them.
---------------------------------------------------------------
The Creeper Relic can be found in the Desert Pyramids.
The Ocelot Relic can be found in the Jungle Pyramids.
And the Guardian Relic can be found in the Ocean Ruins.
---------------------------------------------------------------
You might notice that when you obtain a relic, it will say "unactivated" in its name.
To activate the relic, you must hunt down and kill each relic's corresponding relic monster.
---------------------------------------------------------------
Relic Creeper
The Relic Creeper is a charged creeper, making it easier to spot in a crowd of mobs.
However, the Relic Creeper is very VERY strong.
The Relic Creeper is slow, but has a lot of health.
The Relic Creeper also doesn't take knockback.
The Relic Creeper also will explode almost instantly.
The Relic Creeper can spawn anywhere a regular creeper can spawn.
Relic Ocelot
The Relic Ocelot carries an end crystal on its back.
This makes the Relic Ocelot VERY dangerous and explosive.
The Relic Ocelot is also very fast.
The Relic Ocelot can spawn in jungles, or around villages.
Relic Guardian
The Relic Guardian might look like a regular guardian, but it has a secret.
The Relic Guardian has 2 Relic Guardian Followers that come with it.
The Relic Guardian can only spawn inside of Ocean Monuments, making them difficult to hunt down.
---------------------------------------------------------------
Each Relic Monster upon death will drop a Relic Gem.
Yes, the Relic Ocelot will drop its gem upon blowing up.
The same can't be said for the Relic Creeper though.
--------------------------------------------------------------- Once you have the relic's corresponding gem, you can right click with the relic to add the gem.
Each relic has a right click and a shift right click ability.
---------------------------------------------------------------
Creeper Relic
Right Click: Produce a small/medium explosion that doesn't break blocks. 40 Second Cooldown
Shift+Right Click: Produce a large explosion that will destroy blocks. 60 Second Cooldown
Ocelot Relic
Right Click: Gain a small speed boost that lasts as long as you right click. 0 Second Cooldown
Shift+Right Click: Gain a very high speed boost that lasts for 10 seconds. 40 second Cooldown
Guardian Relic
Right Click: Shoot a laser, you can't move while shooting. 0 Second Cooldown
Shift+Right Click: Shoot a very powerful laser, your health is slowly drained and you can't move. 0 Second Cooldown
