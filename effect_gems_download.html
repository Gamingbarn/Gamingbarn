This datapack adds a new mob to your world called the Gem Zombie.
The Gem Zombie can spawn in place of regular zombies.
So if a zombie can spawn, a Gem Zombie might spawn.
The Gem zombie will always drop one gem.
There are 10 gems that this gem zombie can drop.
Plus, a pearl can be droped too.

To use the gem's powers, put the gem in your off-hand slot.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Here is a full list of every gem and it's effects.

Ruby
-Grants regeneration
Amber
-Grants fire resistance
Topaz
-Grants haste
Aventurine
-Grants night vision
Amethyst
-Grants strength
Sapphire
-Grants water breathing
Aquamarine
-Grants speed
Diopside
-Grants jump boost
Quartz
-Grants glowing
Onyx
-Grants resistance
Pearl
-Grants dolphin's grace
