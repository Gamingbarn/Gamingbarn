This is meant to be a datapack that you put on a world and play in survival.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds a new mob to your world called the Gem Zombie.
The Gem Zombie can spawn in place of regular zombies.
So if a zombie can spawn, a Gem Zombie might spawn.
The Gem zombie will always drop one gem.
There are 10 gems that this gem zombie can drop.
Plus, a pearl can be droped too.
The full list and the effects of each gem are in the picture above next to the thumbnail.
