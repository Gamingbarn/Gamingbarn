There are new mobs you can find in the Nether.
These mobs are known as the Diamond Zombified Piglins.
They are zombified piglins that are covered in diamonds.
When they die they will always drop an unbreakable diamond weapon, tool, or armor.
They can also drop diamonds and even netherite.
They will spawn in place of regular zombified piglins.
So where ever a zombified piglin can spawn.
A Diamond Zombified Piglin can spawn.
The unbreakable diamond armor they drop can even be upgraded to unbreakable netherite.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Be careful though
The piglins have die to drop their unbreakable diamond gear.
And their friends don't like it when you attack them.
