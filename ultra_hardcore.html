This datapack makes almost all monsters stronger.
Each monster comes with its own new strengths.
All affected mobs will spawn as a strong variant.
All monsters that are strong will have a red name.
There is also a hardcore mode that can be turned on or off.
Turn it on with:
/function ultra_hardcore:hardcore_on

Turn if off with:
/function ultra_hardcore:hardcore_off

-------------------------------------------------------------------
Strong Zombie
Has increased health.
Has increased knockback resistance.
Deal more damage.
Increased speed.
Can always break doors.
Strong Husks, Drowned, and, Zombie Villagers all have same stats.
Strong Creeper
Larger explosion.
Explodes INSTANTLY upon contact.
Has increased health.

Zombies, creepers, spiders, and skeletons have a rare chance to spawn as a super strong monster.
Super strong monsters are VERY powerful.
Approach at your own risk.
