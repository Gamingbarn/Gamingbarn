--------------------------------------------------------------
The Harvester is a new mini-boss this datapack adds.
The Harvester is very strong and even has some different attacks.
If you can kill him, he will drop a very special item.
--------------------------------------------------------------
Spawning
To spawn the harvester you must create the scarecrow shown above and throw a golden hoe on top of his head.
This will begin the spawning process.
--------------------------------------------------------------
The Harvester has 150 health and is immune to knockback.
The Harvester has 3 different attacks he can use in battle.
--------------------------------------------------------------
Basic Melee Attack
This is the attack he will use most of the time.
He will run up to you and attack you with his scythe.
He will deal about 1 heart of damage, however he will also inflict the wither effect.
Bash Attack
This attack will be used occasionally.
He will bash the ground around him dealing significant damage to those around him.
He can also damage other entities with this attack.
Scream Attack
This attack will be used occasionally.
He will stand still, look up, and scream as loud as he can.
This attack will break all nearby blocks.
--------------------------------------------------------------
Once you finally kill this beast, he will drop a special item.
This item is called The Harvester's Scythe.
The Harvester's Scythe deals 6 damage to enemies and has a lower cooldown than a sword.
The Harvester's Scythe has all of the functionality of a hoe, and has efficiency 5.
It also has an ability.
When you hold the scythe and shift on top of any grown crops they will be harvested and replanted.
