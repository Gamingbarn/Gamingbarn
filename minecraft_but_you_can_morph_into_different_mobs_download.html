In this datapack, you can morph into different mobs.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
With this datapack you can morph into different mobs.
The first picture shows the full list.
Each mob has some abilities, each mob and its abilities are listed below.

Chicken
-Can float down slowly.
-Has 4 hearts.
Rabbit
-Can jump higher.
-Can move swifter.
-Has 3 hearts.
Snow Golem
-Leaves a trail of snow on the ground.
-Has unlimited snowballs.
-Has a "pumpkin" on his head.
-Has 5 hearts.
Cat
-Can move swifter.
-Has night vision.
-Has 3 hearts.
Slime
-Can jump really high.
-Can move only by jumping.
-Has the same amount of hearts as a human.
Dolphin
-Can swim very fast.
-Can move only by swimming.
-Has 5 hearts.
Cow>
-Has unlimited milk.
-Has 5 hearts.
Pig>
-Has 5 hearts.
Sheep
-Has unlimited wool.
-Has 4 hearts.
Villager
-Gets better deals with other villagers.
-Has 10 hearts.
Enderman
-Has 20 hearts.
-Has unlimited enderpearls.
Skeleton
-Has unlimited bows and arrows.
-Has 10 hearts.
Hoglin
-Has 40 hearts.
-Is immune to knockback.
-Can use his tusks to knock back entities.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The datapack is installed like usual.
