The premise of this datapack is simple.
Touching grass, will instantly kill you.
Upon loading up the world,
the challenge will begin in 5 seconds.
When the challenge begins every player gets a small platform to stand on.
And one player's platform will be the spawn point for everyone.
Touching the sides of grass is okay.
But just don't stand on it.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack can be played with up to 10 people.

If you someone new wants to join the challenge make sure to run the command: /reload !
You will need to do this for every new player!
