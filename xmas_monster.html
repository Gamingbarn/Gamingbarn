This datapack adds in a brand new Christmas-themed boss to your Minecraft world.
He is very strong and has several different attacks!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To spawn him in, you must construct the Christmas tree shown above,
and throw either a golden ingot or a diamond on top.
Depending on which one you add as the "star",
you will spawn either a weaker version, or a stronger version.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Xmas Monster (The weaker version)
This version has 200 Hp and deals 10 damage.
You spawn him in by throwing a golden ingot on top of the tree.
This version will drop a Christmas Present upon death,
the Christmas Present has all sorts of goodies inside,
so make sure to place the present on the ground
so you can have them.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Xmas Monster (The harder version)
This version has a whopping 300 Hp and deals 14 damage.
You spawn him in by throwing a diamond on top of the tree.
This version will drop not one, but 3 Christmas Presents.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Christmas monster can use 3 special custom attacks.
These attacks should not be taken lightly.
Flash Freeze
This attack will instantly freeze the surrounding area.
Causing snow to form everywhere.
All players unfortunate to get caught in this will instantly be encased in powder snow.
Ice Cloud
This attack will spawn a small ice cloud.
This will hurt whoever walks in it, but it will heal the Christmas Monster.
Christmas Present
This attack will spawn a tiny Christmas present.
that will do one of three things after 3 seconds.
One possibility is that it will spawn a singular emerald.
Another, is that is will spawn a creeper that will detonate after another 3 seconds.
And one more possibility is that it will spawn a clone of the Christmas Monster.
This clone is far far weaker and looks slightly different.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Christmas Present
The present that the Xmas Monster drops can contain:
Misc resources such as iron or diamonds
Christmas tree ornaments
Decorative Christmas presents
Minecraft themed plushies
Misc building blocks such as bricks or concrete
And each present is guaranteed a special merry Christmas note
