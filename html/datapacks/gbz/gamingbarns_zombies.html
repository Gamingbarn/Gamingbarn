The average zombie in Minecraft is slow, weak, and forgettable.
But the zombies that this datapack intorduces...
They're anything but.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack completely changes the spawning mechanic for mobs.
No more non-zombie hostile mobs in the overworld.
Most hostile mobs will now spawn as zombies.
All zombies will spawn with more health, more attack damage, and faster speed.
But it doesn't end there.
There is a chance that pigs, cows, sheep, chickens, or even horses will spawn as zombified variants.
HOWEVER!
Zombies are very weak to fire!
They will die very quickly if on fire.
All zombies will also leave a trail of fire when they are on fire.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Don't die.
Dying will infect you with the zombie virus.
As a zombie you are slightly stronger and zombies will no longer attack you.
However, you have slowness, mining fatigue, and constant hunger.
So you may want to cure yourself fast.
Rumor has it that some zombies have even tried to cure themselves.
So they might have a special item on them when they die.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Death isn't the only way to get infected, however.
You also have a 25% chance of getting a minor infection when attacked by a zombie.
The minor infection is similar to the regular infection, but is temporary.
Only lasting for 3 minutes.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Swarm Zombie
-Regular Zombie
Nurse Zombie
-Drops gunpowder.
-Can heal up to 2 nearby zombies.
(When you have Gamingbarn's Guns installed they will drop various medical items.)
(Even cures.)
Psychopath Zombie
-Wields a special sword that it can drop on death.
-Because it has a sword it does more damage.
Scorched Zombie
-Is immune to fire.
(When you have Gamingbarn's Guns installed they will drop slightly more ammo.)
Builder Zombie
-Will build bridges and staircases to get to the nearest player.
Miner Zombie
-Will break blocks near itself to get to the nearest player.
Acid Spitter and Acid Spewer
-Will shoot acid at nearby players
Water Zombie
-All zombies will turn into water zombies if they remain in water for too long.
-They are just as good as regular zombies when on land.
-They are VERY fast when in water.
Water Spitter
-Will spit water projectiles at the nearest player. (These are weaker than the acid spit)
-They are just as good as regular zombies when on land.
-They are VERY fast when in water.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
One more thing to mention.
When you hurt a zombie,
All nearby zombies will come to its aid.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack also has some commands players with operator can run
Run the following commands to turn off/on player infections. (This is on by default)
/function config:infection_off
/function config:infection_on
Run the following commands to turn off/on zombie animals. (This is on by default)
/function config:animal_zombies_off
/function config:animal_zombies_on
Run the following commands to turn off/on zombie builders/miners. (This is on by default)
/function config:special_zombies_off
/function config:special_zombies_on
Run the following command to summon any of the custom zombies (Change [​ZOMBIE] with the type of zombie)
/function summon:[ZOMBIE]
Run the following commands to turn off/on any of the different zombie types! (They are on by default)
/function config:[ZOMBIE_NAME]_off
/function config:[ZOMBIE_NAME]_on
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The books that zombies drop can be scrapped in the crafting grid for 3 rabbit hides.
