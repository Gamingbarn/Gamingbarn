The datapack introduces a new mob called the Gem Zombie,
which can spawn in place of regular zombies in your world.
These Gem Zombies will always drop one gem, with a total of 12 different gems.

To use the gem's powers, put the gem in your off-hand slot.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ruby Effect Gem
-Grants regeneration
Amber Effect Gem
-Grants fire resistance
Topaz Effect Gem
-Grants haste
Aventurine Effect Gem
-Grants night vision
Amethyst Effect Gem
-Grants strength
Sapphire Effect Gem
-Grants water breathing
Aquamarine Effect Gem
-Grants speed
Diopside Effect Gem
-Grants jump boost
Quartz Effect Gem
-Grants glowing
Onyx Effect Gem
-Grants resistance
Pearl
-Grants dolphin's grace
Cordierite Gem
-Grants slow falling
