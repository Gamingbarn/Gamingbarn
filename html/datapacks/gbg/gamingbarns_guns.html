---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds 15 new guns, 4 new medical items, and 4 new equipment items!
Each item has its own upsides and downsides.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You reload guns with shift + right click!
The amount of ammo left in your magazine is displayed
by the durability bar of the gun. Or in the gun's description.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TIP: You can hold down right click to fire the gun as fast as possible.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To see the crafting recipes for the guns you need to craft the Gun Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and an iron ingot the crafting grid to craft one.
To see the crafting recipes for the medical items you need to craft the Medical Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and an apple in the crafting grid to craft one.
To see the crafting recipes for the equipment you need to craft the Equipment Crafting Manual.
You must place a book and gunpowder in the crafting grid to craft one.

Medkit
-Gives regeneration 2 for a short period of time
-Gives instant health 1
-Clears all negative effects
Bandage
-Heals 6 hp over 4 seconds
Regeneration Syringe
-Gives regeneration 2 for 20 seconds
Swiftness Syringe
-Gives speed 4 for 30 seconds
Resistance Syringe
-Gives resistance 1 for 1 minute
Absorption Syringe
-Gives 8 absorption hearts for 10 minutes
Health Boost Syringe
-Gives 4 extra hearts for 6.5 minutes
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
C4
-Drops to the ground when used
-Explodes after 10 seconds
Land Mine
-Drops to the ground when used
-Arms itself after 10 seconds
-Explodes when an entity touches it
-Can be triggered by arrows
Laser Shield
-Gives you an additional 10 hearts when used
-Hearts last for 10 minutes
-Hearts will disappear if you die
Auto Turret
-Drops to the ground when used
-Can choose between shooting at nearby Monsters, Players, or Monsters and Players
-Has 30 HP
You can edit the settings, repair, and pickup the turret by running:
/trigger turret_settingsYou can only edit the turret if you are the owner.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
There are several commands you can run to give yourself the various items in this datapack.
You can get all guns by running the command:
/function give:all_guns
You can get all ammo by running the command:
/function give:all_ammo
You can get all healing items by running the command:
/function give:all_meds
You can get all books by running the command:
/function give:all_books
You can get all equipment by running the command:
/function give:all_equipment
Alternatively
You can give yourself each item individually by running the command:
/function give:[ITEM_NAME]

You can also make players immune to most gunshots by running the command:
/function config:player_damage_off﻿
(For players with cheats only)
