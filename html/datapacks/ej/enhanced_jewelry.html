Enhanced Jewelry adds a brand-new jeweling system to Minecraft!
This system adds in rings, bracelets, and necklaces.
Along with 7 different gems.
Each one granting the jewelry their own special attributes.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
There are 3 tiers of jewelry;
Copper, iron, and gold.
Copper is the weakest, gold is the strongest and iron is in the middle of the road.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Each gem has its own method of being obtained.
Ruby
Has a chance of being dropped by common undead mobs (eg: zombies skeletons)
Emerald
Has a high chance of dropping when an emerald ore is mined.
Amethyst
Has a chance of dropping when a fully grown amethyst cluster is mined.
Quartz
Has a chance of dropping when a nether quartz ore is mined.
Diamond
Has a chance of dropping when diamond ore is mined.
Topaz
Has a chance of being found in desert pyramids and jungle temples.
Sapphire
Has a chance of being found in village houses.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
To make jewelry you must craft a jewelry station, the recipe is in the image galley.
You place the base jewelry in the left slot and place a gem in the center slot.
The crafted item will appear in the far right slot.
WARNING: IF ANY ITEMS ARE IN THE FAR RIGHT SLOT WHEN A CRAFT IS MADE
THEY WILL BE DELETED
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Each gem, when added, will give the jewelry different effects.
