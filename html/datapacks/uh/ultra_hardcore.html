This datapack enhances the power of nearly all mobs.
Each monster has power and unique new abilities.
The affected mobs can be identified by a red name.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
There is also hardcore mode that can be turned on or off.
Turn it on with:
/function ultra_hardcore:hardcore_on

Turn if off with:
/function ultra_hardcore:hardcore_off
(This is off by default)

To revive players who died with hardcore mode enabled run the command
/function config:revive_player

Zombies, creepers, spiders, and skeletons have a rare chance to spawn as a super strong monster.
Super strong monsters are VERY powerful.
Approach at your own risk.
