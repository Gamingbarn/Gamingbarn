---------------------------------------------------------------------
This datapack adds in a brand new mob into your world called the Diamond Piglin.
The Diamond Piglin is a piglin with an unusual fascination with diamond gear.
When the Diamond Piglin dies, he will drop one piece of unbreakable diamond gear.
He also has the chance of dropping diamonds, diamond blocks, and even netherite.
And very seldom, he will drop some special enchanted gear.
He is very difficult to kill, however, since he has strong armor and weapons.
And his nearby friends will come to his aid.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
The unbreakable diamond gear can be upgraded to netherite at a smithing table.
---------------------------------------------------------------------
You can summon the Diamond Piglin manually with the command:
/function summon:diamond_piglin
